Top 5 Personal Tips for Final Fantasy XIV Department of Land and Hand classes

Department of Hand classes are called Crafting Classes | Department of Land classes are called Gathering classes

  1. For Crafting, utilize trial synthesis if you are unsure if your gear is up to scratch
    For Gathering, utilize Field Mastery (for BTN) / Sharp Vision (for MIN) if you cannot reach 100% gatherability (Top Percentage)
  2. For Crafting, Food / Medicine buffs are IMPORTANT in future + endgame crafts and content. Leveling CULnarian and ALChemist are important to make.
    For Gathering, Food buffs are not nearly as important, but can give a boon toward a node that you aren't quite geared for. Utilizing food given from quests is advized if stats aren't up to par.
  3. Crafter leveling is the easiest, yet most tedious.
